The Last Artifact

Project

The Last Artifact is an adventure and puzzle game set in the kingdom of Luzmo. After a cataclysm that separated the region of Maska from the world, five islands now float in the sky. The player takes on the role of Valar, advisor to King Ekoh, tasked with exploring these suspended lands, solving puzzles, and restoring balance to the kingdom.

Personal Feedback

Joining Skytale as a QA Tester for The Last Artifact has been a one-of-a-kind experience—far beyond what I expected from a non-profit association. With over 80 volunteers organized into dedicated teams (design, art, tech, communication, and more), Skytale operates like a full-fledged game studio, driven by passion and professionalism.

What struck me most was the level of organization: professional tools, structured meetings, clear validation processes—nothing is left to chance. Working in this environment has shown me how a multidisciplinary volunteer team can achieve the same level of quality and coordination as established studios.

As a QA Tester, I’ve contributed to the game’s quality while witnessing firsthand the dynamic collaboration between different roles. This experience has reinforced my belief that passion and professionalism go hand in hand.
