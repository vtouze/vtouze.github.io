A journey in R’lyeh

Project

This is the origin-story of the Great Elder Myth. Travel the cave in search of evolution particles in hope to become a true "human" !

Personal Feedback

It was a 3-day game jam with a small dev team. This was my first project on Unity where I focused mainly on the UI. I was able to learn a lot of things like making video/sound settings menus. It also allowed me to learn more about animations and asset integrations on Unity.
