Bloodline Tales: Genesis

Project

The player takes on the role of Akasha, an ancient Egyptian queen who has also become the first vampire. She must go from temple to temple and pass the tests of the gods in order to receive their power and the burden that comes with it.
Once in the temples, the player must solve a number of riddles to gain access to the god's room and retrieve his or her blessing or curse.

Personal Feedback

The project proved challenging due to organizational and communication hurdles. Designers and artists struggled to align their visions, resulting in delays within an already tight production timeline. It was a constant race against time, underscoring the crucial role of effective organization and communication across different departments, as well as the significance of soft skills.

Reflecting on this experience, I gained valuable insights that significantly influenced subsequent projects. I learned to proactively address potential risks early on, ensuring smoother production processes moving forward.
