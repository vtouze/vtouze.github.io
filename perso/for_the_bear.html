For the Beer!

Project

For the Beer! is a game where the player play a knight, trying to grab his losted mug of beer. Do to it, he will have to travel the various regions of the kingdom, while dodging obstacles and objects to finally get his beverage.

Personal Feedback

A game designed during a game jam, in three days, under the theme 'make me laugh'. Working with a motivated (and particularly funny) team, I had the opportunity to learn how to manage last-minute changes. This experience allowed me to develop greater rigor, while keeping in mind the various challenges to offer to players. This game jam reinforced my desire to pursue the Tech aspect in design.
