TOMORROW'S DAY

Project

Dallas, Texas, 1999. Congratulations, James Steel, you are now an agent of Dallas' Internal Security. The security of the United States is at risk; communism is spreading through our streets and threatening our institutions. You must stop it.

Personal Feedback

TOMORROW'S DAY is by far my most ambitious project. It offers a valuable opportunity to enhance my programming skills, presenting me with challenges I’ve never faced before. From more intricate animations to advanced optimization, this project pushes me beyond the scope of what I’ve accomplished in the past. This project also presents a long-term challenge, something I had very little experience with during my studies. It’s teaching me how to manage a long-term project with a team, while also covering aspects like communication, marketing, and much more.
