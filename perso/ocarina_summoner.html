Ocarina Summoner

Project

In Ocarina Summoner the player plays a young musician who finds a magical grimoire containing countless scores allowing you to summon mysterious creatures. His goal is to summon as many monsters as possible by deciphering the work in order to fill his collectible card booklet.

Personal Feedback

It was a 3-day game jam with a small dev team. This was my first project on Unity where I focused mainly on the UI. I was able to learn a lot of things like making video/sound settings menus. It also allowed me to learn more about animations and asset integrations on Unity.
