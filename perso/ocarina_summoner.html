Ocarina Summoner

Project

In Ocarina Summoner the player plays a young musician who finds a magical grimoire containing countless scores allowing you to summon mysterious creatures. His goal is to summon as many monsters as possible by deciphering the work in order to fill his collectible card booklet.

Personal Feedback

This game jam enabled me to develop perhaps my best interfaces to date. The project also enabled me to implement scriptable objects, which I hadn't yet had the opportunity to use on my previous projects. I'm particularly pleased with the concept we were able to develop in just 3 days.
